Case Study

FinTech Dashboard Revamp

Revolutionizing financial data visualization for modern banking with a high-performance, real-time analytics platform.

person Role

Frontend Development, UX/UI Design

business Client

FinTech Solutions Inc.

schedule Timeline

3 Months (Jan - Mar 2024)

flag

The Challenge

FinTech Solutions Inc. faced a critical issue with their legacy dashboard: it was slow, cluttered, and failed to provide actionable insights at a glance. Users reported frustration with load times exceeding 5 seconds and a navigation structure that buried key financial reports.

Our primary goal was to completely overhaul the user experience, reducing cognitive load while simultaneously improving data fetching performance by at least 40%. The design needed to feel trustworthy yet cutting-edge, appealing to both traditional bankers and tech-savvy fintech users.

lightbulb

The Solution

We implemented a modular, widget-based architecture using React and Next.js, allowing users to customize their dashboard views. By leveraging TanStack Query for efficient data caching and server-side rendering, we reduced initial load times to under 800ms.

Visually, we adopted a dark-themed UI with high-contrast accent colors to highlight critical alerts and positive trends. The interface now features "Focus Mode," allowing analysts to drill down into specific datasets without distraction.

  • check_circle Improved load speed by 65% across all devices.
  • check_circle Increased user retention by 25% within the first month of launch.
  • check_circle Seamless integration with 3rd party banking APIs.
